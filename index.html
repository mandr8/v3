The Guardian, 2015

Expansion of the breaking news alert feature to the Guardian site. Help homepage
LBi, BT, 2012

Redesign of BT.com Help section homepage. The key goal behind this redesign was to reduce the number of helpdesk calls about the service problems. To achieve this the page was designed so popular help articles could be easily surfaced and new self-diagnostic flows for troubleshooting common service problems could be easily accessed. In this project I’ve helped design the different support modules and develop and test a flexible layout for the whole page. so each module could render correctly across multiple screen sizes, from large desktop browsers to small smartphone browsers (which are especially important to troubleshoot problems when the home broadband is down).

Tesco.com homepage
LBi, Tesco, 2011

Redesign of Tesco.com homepage. Tesco’s homepage works as an entry point for all the different Tesco department sites, but Tesco wanted to optimise it to the needs of its high number of visitors. The team tackled this redesign project by creating a page layout that gave a strong emphasis to the most visited Tesco sites (Tesco Direct, Tesco Groceries, Tesco ClubCard) whilst still providing a simple way to a vast amount of other Tesco content. During this project I’ve collaborated closely with the designer developing the concepts for the page layout and producing at the end a detailed specification document for Tesco’s development team to use.
